The Freeway Map
Because of course the roads map too
I-8 ≡ I-275
East-west spine. Cross it and you're at the beach in a different world. Same psychological effect every time.
I-15 ≡ I-75
Inland north-south. Less personality, more utility. Gets you to Wesley Chapel the same way the 15 gets you to Miramar.
Coronado Br. ≡ The Bridges
Howard Frankland / Gandy / Skyway. Crossing water to get somewhere cooler. Every single time.
